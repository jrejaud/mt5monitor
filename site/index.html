Questions

Does it place or close trades?

No. EA Sentinel is monitoring-only — it watches your accounts and alerts you. It never trades.

Which brokers / platforms?

Any broker on MetaTrader 5. MT4 support is on the roadmap.

Will it work on my VPS?

Yes — the monitor runs alongside your terminal on any Windows VPS, and a cloud option needs no install.
