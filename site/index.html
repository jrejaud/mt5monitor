Frequently asked questions

The questions every algo trader asks before trusting something near their live account.

    • Does it place or close trades? Can it see my money?

      No to both. EA Sentinel never sends an order, never touches your strategy, and doesn’t publish your account anywhere. It only watches that the terminal and your EA are running, and restarts them when they’re not.

    • Isn’t this free with FX Blue, or built into MetaTrader’s VPS?

      Those restart the terminal when the broker connection drops, but a reboot often leaves your EA stripped off the chart, or the terminal looks “connected” while the EA has silently stopped. EA Sentinel watches the EA itself, puts it back on its chart with its settings, and pings your phone.

    • What can’t it fix?

      Honest answer: things outside the terminal. If your broker disables AutoTrading server-side, or your strategy itself is losing, that’s not something a watchdog should override, and we won’t pretend to. We handle terminal/VPS down, EA stopped, freezes and disconnects.

    • Which brokers and platforms?

      Any broker on MetaTrader 4 or 5. The agent runs alongside your terminal on any Windows VPS, including your broker’s or MetaTrader’s own VPS.

    • Do I need to be technical to set it up?

      No. Drop one script on your chart, paste a token, and you’re live in a few minutes. The free tier needs nothing but an email.

    • How do I get started?

      Start with the free monitor, watch one EA and get an email if it goes silent. Upgrade to Pro when you want auto-restart, more accounts, and Telegram/SMS alerts.
