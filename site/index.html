Questions

Does it place or close trades? Can it see my money?

No to both. EA Sentinel never sends an order, never touches your strategy, and doesn't publish your account anywhere. It only watches that the terminal and your EA are running, and restarts them when they're not.

Isn't this free with FX Blue, or built into MetaTrader's VPS?

Those restart the terminal when the broker connection drops — but a reboot often leaves your EA stripped off the chart, or the terminal looks "connected" while the EA has silently stopped. EA Sentinel watches the EA itself (a per-EA heartbeat), puts it back on its chart with its settings, and pings your phone — without you installing three tools or publishing your account to a third-party site.

What can't it fix?

Honest answer: things outside the terminal. If your broker disables AutoTrading server-side, or your strategy itself is losing, that's not something a watchdog should override — and we won't pretend to. We handle terminal/VPS down, EA stopped, freezes and disconnects.

Which brokers / platforms?

Any broker on MetaTrader 5. MT4 support is on the roadmap.

Will it work on my VPS?

Yes — the agent runs on any Windows VPS alongside your terminal, including your broker's or MetaTrader's own VPS. If you're not on a VPS yet, we'll point you at one.
