Questions

Does it place or close trades?

Never. EA Sentinel doesn't touch your strategy logic or send a single order. It only keeps the platform and your EA running — restarting, reconnecting and re-attaching when they fail.

How is this different from FxBlue Watcher?

FxBlue's watcher mainly alerts and publishes. EA Sentinel actually performs the recovery — restarting the VPS and terminal, relaunching EAs on boot, and clearing CPU-spike freezes — across multiple VPSs from one dashboard.

Which brokers / platforms?

Any broker on MetaTrader 5. MT4 support is on the roadmap.

Will it work on my VPS?

Yes — the agent runs on any Windows VPS alongside your terminal. If you're not on a VPS yet, we'll point you at one.
